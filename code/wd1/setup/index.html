In Radiant: Offline Battle Arena the strategy begins before the first battle. During the setup phase you will be drafting your champions for the game ahead. Here you will have an opportunity to make your ideal composition, or sabotage your opponent's plans to construct theirs. Even if things have not gone your way during the hero draft you will have the opportunity to round out your team with the ideal deity and items to counter your enemy's plans.

Setup Steps

  1. Determine Play Order
  2. Draft Champions
  3. Choose Deity and Items
  4. Place Conduits
  5. Draw Initial Hand
  6. Place Champions

Determine Play Order

Play order is determined by the flip of a coin. The winner of the flip chooses whether to go first. From this point forward, the first and second players will be referred to as Player A and Player B respectively.

Player A will only have 2 action points in their first turn. Consider the choice to go first carefully. There are both pros and cons for going first in ROBA!

Draft Champions

In this phase players take turns picking champions for their team. Your aim is to both make a synergistic team and to try to counter the synergies in your opponent's team.

More advanced players may choose to implement bans in their drafts. A banned champion cannot be chosen by either player.

  1. Player A Picks
  2. Player B Picks
  3. Player A Bans (optional)
  4. Player B Bans (optional)
  5. Player B Picks
  6. Player A Picks
  7. Player A Picks
  8. Player B Picks

Once you have drafted three champions each you build your deck. To do this you take the 10 cards for each of your champions and shuffle them together.

Your Heroes can use any of your Champion's cards - they are not locked by the source Champion. Take this into account when drafting to build a synergistic team.

It is advised that you draft one evoker for the free point of power each turn. It is also advised to draft at least one champion with an attack of 3 or higher to make sure you can easily threaten enemy conduits.

Choose Deity and Items

After both Champion teams are drafted you can select your Deity and Items. Each player selects 1 Deity and 3 Items. You select these in secret.

You do not equip items when you select them at this stage. They will comprise your shop, which you will buy from in the end phase

Each Deity card has a conduit icon on the bottom left. This icon indicates which conduits you must select.

Choose your Deity and Items to round your team out - aim to fill any gaps in your team or to exploit gaps in your opponent's.

Place Conduits

Starting with Player A, each player will take turns placing their conduits one at a time. Conduits should be placed with the Divine Conduit face up.

Take care not to reveal the ruined face of the conduit to your opponent.

The ruin effects printed on the other side of the cards will trigger when they are destroyed, either by combat or by sacrifice..

Draw Initial Hand

Each player should now draw their initial hand of five cards from their deck. There are no mulligans when drawing your initial hand - you get what you get!

Place Champions

In the final step of the setup phase you will take turns placing your Champions, starting from Player A.

If someone has chosen The Wrathlord as their Deity, they manifest it after all champions have been placed.
