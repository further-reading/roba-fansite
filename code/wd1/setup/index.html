In Radiant: Offline Battle Arena the strategy begins before the first battle. During the setup phase you will be drafting your heroes for the game ahead. Here you will have an opportunity to make your ideal composition, or sabotage your opponenet's plans to construct theirs. Even if things have not gone your way during the hero draft you will have the opportunity to round out your team with the ideal diety and items to counter your enemy's plans.

Setup Steps

  1. Determine Play Order
  2. Draft Heroes
  3. Choose Diety and Items
  4. Board Placement

Determine Play Order

Play order is determined by the flip of a coin. The winner of the flip chooses whether to go first. From this point forward, the first and second players will be referred to as Player A and Player B respectively.

Player A will only have 2 Action Points in their first turn. Consider the choice to go first carefully. There are both pros and cons for going first in ROBA!

Hero Draft

In this phase players take turns picking Heroes for their team. Your aim is to both make a synergistic team and to try to counter the synergies in your opponent's team.

More advanced players may choose to implement bans in their drafts. A banned hero cannot be chosen by either player.

  1. Player A Picks
  2. Player B Picks
  3. Player A Bans (optional)
  4. Player B Bans (optional)
  5. Player B Picks
  6. Player A Picks
  7. Player A Picks
  8. Player B Picks

Choose Diety and Items

After both Hero teams are drafted you can select your Diety and Items. Each player selects 1 Diety and 3 Items. You select these in secret.

Each Diety card has a conduit icon on the bottom left. This icon indicates which conduits you must select.

Choose your Diety and Items to round your team out - aim to fill any gaps in your team or to exploit gaps in your opponent's.

Board Placement

Finally, each player will take turns placing their conduits and then Heroes on the board one at a time. Once all condits and heroes are on the board the game can begin!

If someone has chosen The Wraithlord as their Diety, you manifest it at this point - after all conduits and heroes have been placed but before the first turn begins.
