The end phase occurs at the end of every turn. In this phase you have final steps you take to conclude your turn.

Actions

  1. Revive Step
  2. Shop Step
  3. Draw Step

Revive Step

In the revive step you can choose to revive your KO'd champions. You have two methods to do so.

First you can choose to ruin a conduit. Ruining a conduit will revive all K.O.ed champions. You can immediately take them from the graveyard and place them on any lanes you choose.

Your conduit's ruin effect will still trigger, and if it is your last conduit your deity will manifest.

Second, you can spend 5 power on your one-time buyback. This will revive one champion. After using your one-time buyback you should flip the card around to indicate that it has been used.

Shop Step

During the shop step you may choose to purchase any of the items you selected during the setup phase.

Every hero can only have 1 item equipped. When you buy the item, any equip effects will also trigger.

A hero cannot have two items equipped. You can choose to replace an item equipped by a hero, but doing so banishes the item.

Draw Step

The final act you take to end your turn is the Draw Step. For this step you draw a card from the top of your deck and add it to your hand.

If you cannot draw a card during this step you lose the game.
