Banish

A banished card is permanently removed from the game.

Blocking

A blocking hero is intercepting an attack targeting an ally of conduit in the same lane. Blocking changes the target to the hero, reduces them to 0 attack and commits them.

Champion

A champion is a mortal hero selected during the draft phase.

Clash

A clash refers to playing a card during the clash step of a combat.

Clash Effects

A clash effect activates when a card is played during a clash in combat.

Combat Damage

Combat damage refers to damage dealt during a combat.

Committed

A committed hero has 0 attack and cannot take actions that would commit them. They can be recovered using an action.

Cooldown

Cooldowns can only be used once per turn.

Deck

Your deck is a set of 30 skill cards. Your champions determine your deck.

Deities

Deities are a special type of hero. They are chosen in secret at the start of the game. A player loses is their deity is K.O.ed

Discard

Discarded cards are placed in players' discard piles. Cards are discarded when played as clashes or talents. Some special abilities may also caused cards to become discarded

Divine

A divine hero can use ultimate effects without being levelled up and are not considered mortal. By default, all deities are divine and all champions are mortal.

Evoker

Evokers are a type of hero. They generate one power during the preparation phase provided they are not KOed.

Graveyard

The graveyard is where heroes are when they are KOed.

Hero

Heroes can be champions or deities.

K.O.ed

A hero is K.O.ed when they are reduced to 0 health.

Manifest

A deity enters the field by manifesting. A manifestation can occur when all a player's conduits are ruined or if the manifest action is taken.

Manifesting causes an allied hero to become banished

Mortal

Mortal heroes are not divine. By default, all deities are divine and all champions are mortal.

Opposing

Opposing heroes refer to enemy heroes participating in a combat.

Piercing

Piercing damage cannot be blocked by shields.

Power

Power is a currency earned and spent in various ways.

Skills

Skill cards are a set of 10 cards unique to a given champion. The are used to create your deck.

Solo Attack

A solo attack describes an attack where the attacker is only using one hero.

Stun

Stun damage cannot KO a hero - heroes afflicted by stun damage cannot have their health reduce below 1.

Talent Effect

A talent effect triggers when a card is played as a talent action during the action phase.

Team Attack

A team attack describes an attack where the attacker is using multiple heroes.

Wounds

Wound tokens are placed on heroes and conduits when they are damaged. Each wound token reduces their current health by one.

Ultimate

Ultimate clash effects and ultimate talent effects can only be triggered by divine or levelled up heroes.

Vanguard

A hero with vanguard must be targeted by the defender in a team attack.
